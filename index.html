Our Core Services

NOWA combines finance and mobility to create a truly connected community experience.

The NOWA Digital Credits Ecosystem

A stable, digital credit system designed for local trade within Nakuru, ensuring money stays within the community to fuel local businesses and opportunities.

For Users

  • Accessible: Easily top-up credits at local agents.
  • Safe: Pay instantly and securely using a simple mobile app.
  • Incentivized: Earn rewards for local purchases.

For Merchants

  • Zero Hidden Fees: Low, transparent transaction costs.
  • Instant Settlement: Payments credited immediately; no waiting time.
  • Wider Customer Base: Access to the growing NOWA user community.

The Seamless Transport Platform

A cashless, organized system for booking and paying for local transport services (matatus, taxis, and boda-bodas), making mobility safer and more efficient.

Safety & Convenience

  • Safety First: Vetted drivers and GPS tracking for all journeys.
  • Fair Fares: Transparent, standardized pricing; no cash haggling.
  • Convenience: Book and pay directly using NOWA digital credits.

Driver Support

  • Stability: Digital tools for organized route management.
  • Training: Resources for professional development and service standards.
  • Earning: Predictable, stable income through cashless payments.

Our Commitment to Nakuru

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower the Nakuru community by providing an inclusive, stable, and accessible digital ecosystem for local commerce and safe, efficient mobility.

Vision: A financially resilient and digitally connected Nakuru where opportunity, economic stability, and safe transport are accessible to every resident.
